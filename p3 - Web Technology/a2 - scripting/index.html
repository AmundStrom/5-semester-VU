Kanye Grammy Awards

Kanye west has a mind-boggling 24 Grammy Award Wins for a combination of singles and albums between 2005 and 2022. Here are top 15 Grammy Award Wins from Kanye West over the years:

Year 2005
- Best Rap Album for The College Dropout
- Best R&B Song Grammy Award
Year 2006
- Best Rap Album for Late Registration
- Best Rap Solo Performance Grammy Award
Year 2008
- Best Rap Album for Graduation
- Best Rap Song Grammy Award
Year 2009
- Best Rap Performance by a Duo or Group Grammy Award
Year 2012
- Best Rap Album for My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy
- Best Rap Performance Grammy Award
- Best Rap/Sung Collaboration Grammy Award
Year 2013
- Best Rap/Sung Collaboration Grammy Award
- Best Rap Performance Grammy Award
Year 2021
- Best Contemporary Christian Music Album for Jesus Is King
Year 2022
- Best Rap Song Grammy Award
- Best Melodic Rap Performance Grammy Award
